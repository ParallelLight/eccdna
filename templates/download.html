Users can download eccDNA data from TeCD.

Datasets download

  All data searched by users on the search page can be downloaded. According to the classification, we provide the documents of two organisms: saccharomyces cerevisiae and homo sapiens. Users can download it according to their own needs.

  After downloading the datasets, you can see that the data results are in Excel (The following figures show parts of the data in the CSV file from TeCD).

  You can go to the about page, when you want to know the meaning of each column.
